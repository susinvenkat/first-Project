Our Story

A true first-generation entrepreneur, Muthukumar Sunderrajan laid the foundation of this empire in 1992 with "SUMKA SONS", starting as a small trading company with no prior industry experience or family business background. Through dedication, innovation, and commitment to excellence, we have grown into a leading manufacturer of industrial actuators and gearboxes.

Today, we serve customers worldwide across diverse industries, providing reliable motion control solutions for critical applications. Our journey from a small trading company to an established industrial manufacturer reflects our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To provide world-class motion control solutions that enhance operational efficiency, reliability, and safety for our customers across all industries. We strive to be the preferred partner for industrial automation and control systems.
