About Susin I-Tork India

Susin I-Tork India specialises in providing reliable flow-control and valve automation solutions in challenging industrial environments. Founded in 2007 as a strategic joint venture with I-TORK Controls (South Korea), the organisation initially focused on marketing I-TORK actuators and later transitioned to manufacturing pneumatic actuators locally.

The facility underwent significant expansion in 2017 and now serves major industries including power generation, cement, sugar, chemical processing, and energy sectors. Since 2020, Susin I-Tork India has been successfully exporting pneumatic actuators from its Indian manufacturing plants to international markets.

Key Focus: Manufacturing excellence in pneumatic actuators with emphasis on quality, reliability, and customer-specific solutions for critical industrial applications.
