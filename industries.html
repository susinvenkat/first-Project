Our Industry Solutions

Oil & Gas

SUSIN is a leading provider of Valve Automation Applications across the upstream, midstream, and downstream sectors in the Asian market. Our exceptional track record is highlighted by approvals from major refineries, showcasing our commitment to quality and reliability. We have over 100 actuators in successful operation within FPSO projects, solidifying our expertise and trustworthiness in critical oil & gas industry applications.

Key Applications:

  • Pipeline valve control
  • Refinery process control
  • Wellhead automation
  • Emergency shutdown systems

Water Treatment

Reliable actuators for water and wastewater treatment facilities, ensuring precise control of valves and gates in critical water management processes. Our solutions meet stringent water quality and environmental standards.

Key Applications:

  • Water distribution networks
  • Wastewater treatment plants
  • Pumping stations
  • Reservoir control

Power Generation

SUSIN has proudly provided steadfast support to the power industry for over 15 years, earning approvals from esteemed organizations such as BHEL, NTPC, and Kahramaa in Qatar. Currently supporting 100+ power industries across Asia, we deliver reliable valve automation solutions for thermal, nuclear, and renewable energy facilities where operational excellence is critical.

Key Applications:

  • Steam turbine control
  • Boiler feedwater systems
  • Cooling water systems
  • Nuclear safety systems
