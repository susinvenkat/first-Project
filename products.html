Pneumatic Scotch Yoke Rotary Actuators

Series: PDS Actuator, HD Actuator

High-performance pneumatic actuators featuring the scotch yoke mechanism for reliable rotary motion control. Designed for demanding industrial applications requiring precise positioning and high torque output.

Key Features

  • Scotch yoke mechanism for smooth, reliable operation
  • High torque output with compact design
  • Corrosion-resistant materials and coatings
  • Wide range of rotation angles (90°, 180°, custom)
  • Optional position feedback and limit switches
  • Suitable for harsh industrial environments

Applications

Valve Automation
Process Control
Material Handling
Packaging Machinery

Technical Specifications

Torque Range: 50 Nm to 50,000 Nm
Operating Pressure: 2 to 10 bar
Rotation Angle: 90°, 180°, or Custom
Temperature Range: -20°C to +80°C
Materials: Aluminum, Stainless Steel, Carbon Steel

Electro-Hydraulic Actuators

Advanced actuators that combine electrical control with hydraulic power, delivering high-force output and precise positioning for critical industrial applications.

Key Features

  • High force output with compact design
  • Precise positioning and control
  • Integrated control electronics
  • Fail-safe operation options
  • Low maintenance requirements
  • Suitable for harsh environments

Applications

Large Valve Control
Heavy Machinery
Offshore Platforms
Power Plants

Technical Specifications

Force Range: 5 kN to 500 kN
Stroke Length: 50 mm to 2000 mm
Operating Pressure: Up to 210 bar
Control Signal: 4-20 mA, 0-10V, Digital
Position Accuracy: ±0.1% of stroke

Electrical Actuators

Series: ITQ Series (Rotary)

Clean, efficient, and reliable electric actuators offering precise linear and rotary motion control with integrated control systems for modern automation applications.

Key Features

  • Clean operation - no compressed air or hydraulic fluid
  • High precision positioning
  • Integrated control and feedback
  • Energy efficient operation
  • Easy installation and maintenance
  • Network connectivity options (Modbus, Profibus, etc.)

Applications

Process Automation
Water Treatment
HVAC Systems
Manufacturing

Technical Specifications

Torque Range: 10 Nm to 10,000 Nm
Voltage: 24V DC, 110V AC, 230V AC, 380V AC
Speed: Adjustable, typically 5-60 rpm
Position Accuracy: ±0.1%
Protection Class: IP65, IP67, IP68
