Susin I-Tork India

About

Susin I-Tork India specialises in providing reliable flow-control and valve automation solutions in challenging environments. Founded in 2007 as a joint venture with I-TORK Controls (South Korea), the organisation initially focused on marketing I-TORK actuators and later moved to manufacture pneumatic actuators locally. The facility expanded in 2017 and now serves major industries including power, cement, sugar, chemical and energy. Susin I-Tork India has been exporting pneumatic actuators from its Indian plants since 2020.

Milestones

  • 2007 — JV established with I-TORK Controls; early market entry into valve automation.
  • 2010–2012 — Began manufacturing pneumatic actuators in India (initial SKD to full local manufacture).
  • 2017 — Moved to larger premises and widened product range.
  • 2020 — Began exporting actuators from Indian plants.
  • Selected Projects — deliveries and service across India and international projects including power plants, desalination, and FLNG/FPSO installations.
