About Susin I-Tork UAE

Susin I-Tork ME FZC serves as the Middle East regional headquarters, strategically located in Sharjah's SAIF Zone free trade area. We provide comprehensive valve automation solutions across the UAE and wider Middle East region.

Our UAE facility specializes in distributing pneumatic actuators, electric actuators, and gearboxes for critical applications in oil & gas, petrochemical, power generation, and water treatment industries. We maintain strategic stock to ensure rapid delivery across the GCC region.

Strategic Location: Located in SAIF Zone Sharjah, providing efficient logistics and distribution across UAE, Qatar, Oman, and the broader Middle East market.
