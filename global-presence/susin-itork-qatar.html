About Susin Qatar

Susin Qatar Trading & Contracting W.L.L has been serving Qatar's industrial sector for over 15 years, specializing in valve automation solutions for the power generation industry. We are proud to be an approved supplier for Kahramaa, Qatar's primary electricity and water utility.

Our Qatar operations focus on providing high-quality pneumatic and electric actuators for critical infrastructure projects including power plants, water treatment facilities, and industrial automation applications. We maintain strong relationships with major EPC contractors and end-users throughout Qatar.

Industry Recognition: Approved vendor for Kahramaa and major power generation projects. Trusted partner for NTPC and other leading organizations in Qatar's energy sector.
