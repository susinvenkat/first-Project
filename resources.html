Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Scotch Yoke Actuator?

A Scotch Yoke actuator is a type of pneumatic rotary actuator that uses a scotch yoke mechanism to convert linear motion into rotary motion. It provides high torque output with a compact design, making it ideal for valve automation and other industrial applications.

How do I select the right actuator for my application?

Actuator selection depends on several factors including required torque, operating pressure, rotation angle, environmental conditions, and valve type. Our technical team can assist you in selecting the optimal actuator for your specific application. Please refer to our Product Selection Guide or contact our engineering team.

What is the difference between pneumatic and electric actuators?

Pneumatic actuators use compressed air for operation and are ideal for applications requiring high speed and force. Electric actuators use electric motors and offer precise positioning, integrated control, and clean operation without compressed air. The choice depends on your specific requirements, available utilities, and application needs.

Do you provide custom solutions?

Yes, we offer customization services including special mounting configurations, custom rotation angles, special materials, and application-specific modifications. Our engineering team works closely with customers to develop tailored solutions for unique requirements.

What certifications do your products have?

Our products are certified to various international standards including ISO 9001:2015, CE marking, ATEX for hazardous areas, and API standards for oil and gas applications. Specific certifications vary by product model and application.

What kind of support do you provide?

We provide comprehensive support including technical assistance, installation and commissioning services, training programs, maintenance support, spare parts supply, and 24/7 emergency support. Our service team is available to assist you throughout the product lifecycle.
